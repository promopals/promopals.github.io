About Promo Pals Bali

In many places like Bali, there is an abundance of choice when it comes to food, and it is ever-changing. Choosing where to eat is not always easy, even for locals. Most establishments offer weekly promotions and special deals at specific times or on particular days of the week. Combining these two facts with the fact that prices for eating out are going up is what birthed Promo Pals.

We are here to help you make better decisions on where to go and when, to have yourself a healthier wallet by the end of the week.

We rely on our community for tips about ongoing offers and serve you back with an easy-to-use interface for finding your perfect match. So we are ready to help whether you are looking for a taco Tuesday, a fix for your pizza craving, an upcoming Friday happy hour, or just a good reason to try a new place.

A good deal just tastes better.
